Biography

I am a researcher at Intelligent Imaging group at Samsung Research America. My research interests are primarily in the overlapping areas of image/video processing, computer vision and computational photography. I also interested in applying self-supervision and generative modeling techniques to various imaging applications. Previously, I did my PhD in the beautiful city of Austin, TX, at UT Austin under the ‘great’ Dr. Alan Bovik’s supervision!

You can find my resume here.
